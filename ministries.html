CRC Kids

A place where
kids encounter God.

CRC Kids is a safe, joyful, and Spirit-filled environment where children from 2 to 12 years old can discover who God is in a way that makes sense to them. We believe the next generation is not the church of tomorrow — they are the church of today.

Every Sunday, our trained volunteers create an experience that's age-appropriate, Bible-based, and genuinely fun. Kids don't just learn about God — they experience Him.

  • Sunday services at 08:00 & 10:00 — running parallel to the main service
  • Age-based classes from toddlers through to Grade 6
  • Safe check-in and check-out system for every child
  • Trained volunteers with a heart for the next generation
  • Bible-based curriculum with worship, teaching and activities

CRC Youth

Real faith
for real life.

CRC Youth is a space where teenagers can be completely themselves — the doubts, the questions, the pressure — and still encounter a God who is real. We don't want youth who just attend church. We want young people who are on fire, rooted in the Word, and ready to impact their generation.

Through powerful services, authentic community, and leaders who genuinely care, CRC Youth gives teenagers a place to belong and a reason to believe.

  • Junior Youth for Grade 5 to Grade 7 — bridging kids and main youth
  • Senior Youth for Grade 8 and up — real conversations, real God
  • Youth services with worship, teaching and community
  • Youth leaders who invest in every individual
  • Regular youth events, camps and gatherings

CRC Women

Women who know
who they are.

CRC Women is a community where women are celebrated, strengthened, and sent. Whether you're navigating motherhood, career, marriage, or simply the everyday demands of life — this is a space where you will find women who have walked that road and a God who walks it with you.

Through conferences, homecells, and community gatherings, CRC Women builds friendships that go deeper than Sunday and cultivates women who know their identity, their purpose, and their God.

  • Women's homecells for connection and discipleship
  • Annual conferences and special events
  • A Mothers Room available during services
  • Community built around real relationships
  • Teaching that is practical, powerful and grounded in the Word

CRC Music

Worship that
moves people.

Music at CRC Worcester is not a performance — it is a ministry. Our worship team exists to create an environment where the presence of God is tangible and where people encounter Him through sound, song, and surrender.

If you have a gift for music, vocals, sound, or media — we believe that gift was given to be used for the Kingdom. CRC Music is always looking for people who are passionate about worship and willing to serve with excellence.

  • Live worship at every Sunday service
  • A team of musicians, vocalists and creatives serving together
  • Sound, media and visual production behind every service
  • Regular rehearsals and team development
  • Open to anyone with a gift and a willing heart

CRC Cares

Love that
shows up.

CRC Cares is where the mission of the church meets the streets of Worcester. We believe the church is not called to sit inside its walls — it is called to go out into the sea of humanity and serve with compassion, dignity, and the love of God.

Through community outreach projects, food drives, and hands-on service, CRC Cares puts love into action. Every initiative is an expression of our belief that people matter — not because of what they can do, but because of who God says they are.

  • Community outreach projects across Worcester
  • Food and essentials distribution for families in need
  • Volunteer opportunities open to every church member
  • Partnerships with local organisations serving the community
  • A heart for the marginalised, the forgotten, and the overlooked
