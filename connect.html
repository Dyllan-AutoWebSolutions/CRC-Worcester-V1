First time?

Here's what to expect.

01

Arrive & be welcomed

From the moment you pull into the parking lot, someone will be there to greet you. Our welcome team will point you in the right direction and make sure you feel at home before you even walk through the door.

02

Experience the service

Expect powerful worship music, a relevant and practical message from the Bible, and a room full of people who are genuinely glad you're there. Services run approximately 75 to 90 minutes.

03

Kids are taken care of

CRC Kids runs at both Sunday services. Drop your children off at the Kids entrance before the service begins — they'll be safe, cared for, and having the time of their lives while you focus on the message.

04

No pressure, just welcome

You won't be asked to stand up, introduce yourself, or give money on your first visit. Come, experience, and take it at your own pace. We're just glad you showed up.

05

Connect afterwards

After the service, grab a coffee and meet some of our team. If you have questions, want to find out more, or just need someone to talk to — we'll be around and we'd love to chat.

06

Take your next step

When you're ready, the next step is simple — join a homecell, get connected to a ministry, or come back the following Sunday. There's no rush and no pressure. We'll be here.
