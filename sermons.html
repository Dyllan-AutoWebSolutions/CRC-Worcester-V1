2026 Theme Series

A wave of love invading our world.

In 2016, the first wave of Invasion swept through CRC. A decade later, a new wave is building on the shoreline. Invasion 2026 — The Second Wave — is a call to carry a wave of love into every space we occupy: our families, our city, our workplaces, our world.

Based on John 13:34–35, this series unpacks what it looks like to love one another the way Jesus loved — not as a feeling, but as a force. A force that invades everything it touches and leaves nothing unchanged.
